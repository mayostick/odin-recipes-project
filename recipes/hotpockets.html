Hotpockets

Description

Everyones favorite dish made easy, even a Caveman can do it! The main way to make this is the preperation. You can find the steps below!

  • 2 hotpockets
  • ranch dressing of your choice

Ok so now he have the ingredients, lets look at our three easy steps

  1. Cook both hotpockets for 2 minutes
  2. When its done,use a fork to poke a few holes in the top
  3. Lastly cook for another minute and let sit out for 2-3 minutes

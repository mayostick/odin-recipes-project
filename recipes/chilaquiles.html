Chilaquiles

Description

Chilaquiles is a delicous yet simple morning dish that everyone should try. Originating from Mexico, Chilaquiles is a blend eggs, tortillas, mexican cheese, onions and green chile. Many more ingredients such as avocado and chorizo can be added at the chefs demands. This recipes serving size will be for one person.

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 fried corn tortillas
  • Mexican cheese
  • green chile salsa
  1. Cut up the corn tortillas in the style of tortilla chips, next fry the chips.
  2. Fry the eggs to over-medium, or your desired instruction.
  3. Lay cooked chips on a plate, add green chile salsa and cheese, then lay the eggs on top.
